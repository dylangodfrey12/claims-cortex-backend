### Extract Relevant Roofing and Siding Measurements

- Manually review the measurement reports from Hover to locate the applicable roofing and siding measurements.
- The required measurements are typically found in categorized tables within these reports.

---

### For Hover, search for tables with the following measurements:

- Roof Facets
- (Ridges / Hips) (note / is not divided symbol, (Ridges / Hips) is the name of the entire variable)
- Valleys
- Rakes
- Gutters / Eaves
- Flashing
- Step Flashing
- Roof Pitch Area

---

### For Hover, search for tables with the following measurements:

- Facades - Siding
- SIDING PER ELEVATION
    - Total Front
    - Total Right
    - Total Left
    - Total Back
